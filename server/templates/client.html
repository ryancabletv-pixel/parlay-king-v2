❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Gold Standard System

When are picks published?

Picks are generated every morning at 1:00 AM AST by the V3 Titan XII engine and published to the dashboard by 4:00 AM AST. A retry cascade runs at 1:30, 2:00, 2:30, and 3:00 AM to ensure picks are always available.

What is the 68% confidence threshold?

Every pick must score at least 68% on our 12-factor model before it is published. This strict filter removes low-value picks and ensures only high-conviction opportunities reach the dashboard.

What sports are covered?

We cover European Soccer (Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1), NBA Basketball, and MLS (Major League Soccer). Each sport has a dedicated 3-leg parlay and individual pick analysis.

How is the win rate calculated?

Win rate is calculated from all settled (non-pending) Official System Play picks. Pending picks are excluded until the game result is confirmed. The current record is tracked live in the Results tab.

How do I upgrade my membership?

Click the Account tab in the navigation menu to manage your membership tier. Free, Pro ($29/mo), and Lifetime Royalty ($499 one-time) options are available.
